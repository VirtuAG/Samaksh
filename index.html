FAQs

Everything you need to know your consultation. Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Please fill the form below.
Get in touch
How can I decide which therapy plan is best for me?
It is true that for different problem’s different number of sessions are required, to make a decision which therapy plan suits best for you.

First take a single consultation session in which you can understand your therapist and your problem. It will help you to take the decision.
I don’t need any medication can I also start with therapy?
Yes, you can. Medication and therapy both are treatment modalities belonging from different set just like allopathy and homeopathy. Though it is advisable to have a consultation session with your therapist and then take this decision after having a complete knowledge about your  problems.

You have all the rights to choose the treatment modalities and no-one can force you.
If I bought the therapy plan and then unable to attend any session will I be charged for it?
No, if you cancel your session 24 hours prior to your session no charges will be deducted and that missed session will not be counted. Though it is advisable to be regular in your session for better results.
How can therapy help me changing my thoughts or behavior?
Sometimes some of us are aware of the maladaptive behavior pattern we have and sometimes not. Even when we want to stop something we don’t know how to do it. Therapy is a treatment in which we discuss the cause of the problem and how to solve such problems with a solution that best suits you with the help of guided discovery.
